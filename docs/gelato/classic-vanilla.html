01 — INTRODUCTION

There is no quieter test of an ice cream maker than vanilla. No color to hide behind, no texture to lean on. Just dairy, sugar, and the pod.

03 — THE POD

From Sava, by hand

We source only from the Sava region of northeastern Madagascar, where the Bourbon variety was first cultivated. Pods are hand-picked at peak ripeness, sun-cured for three months, and graded by weight before shipping. We reject roughly a third on arrival.

04 — METHOD

  1. 01

    Split and scrape

    Each pod is split lengthwise; the seeds are scraped and reserved. The empty pods go into cold cream.

  2. 02

    Steep

    Cream and pods steep together for 72 hours at 4°C. The seeds are added in the final 12 hours.

  3. 03

    Strain and churn

    The cream is strained through fine mesh. The base is churned slowly at -2°C for 25 minutes — half the speed of a standard batch.

  4. 04

    Rest

    The finished gelato rests in the hardening cabinet for at least 6 hours before service.
